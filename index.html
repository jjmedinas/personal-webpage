About

I'm a software engineer who cares as much about the business as the code. Most of my work lives on the backend — designing systems, APIs, and the quiet infrastructure that makes a product dependable.

But I've never been satisfied stopping at "it works." I like building SaaS products end to end: talking to customers, shaping the roadmap, and making the product and design calls that engineers often hand off to someone else.

What I enjoy most is the zero-to-one moment — turning a rough idea into something people rely on. Balancing engineering with business and design decisions is where I do my best work.
